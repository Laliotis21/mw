# Market Phases — what to look at, and when

The trading day is split into 4 phases. Each phase changes WHAT matters in the
candles and context. The phase is given to you per cycle.

> Crypto (`*-USD`) trades 24/7 and FX (`*=X`) 24/5 — for them, treat the phase
> as "which part of the global session we're in" rather than a hard open/close.

## `pre_market` — before the bell
- **Goal:** read overnight + early signals, form a bias, prepare a plan.
- **Candles:** look at the previous session's close, the overnight/extended
  range, and the gap (today's first prints vs prior close).
- **Decide:** lean toward the gap direction only if backed by volume and a clean
  level break. Otherwise HOLD and wait for the open to confirm.

## `open` — the opening drive (first ~30–60 min)
- **Goal:** trade the opening momentum or its rejection.
- **Candles:** opening range (first few bars), volume spike vs average, whether
  price holds above/below VWAP and the opening range high/low.
- **Decide:** BUY on a confirmed breakout of the opening range with volume; SELL
  on a confirmed breakdown. Fade only with a clear rejection wick + volume.

## `mid_day` — the trend / chop
- **Goal:** trade continuation, or stand aside in low-volume chop.
- **Candles:** trend structure (higher highs/lows or lower), pullbacks to a
  moving average, shrinking range = chop warning.
- **Decide:** BUY pullbacks in an uptrend / SELL pullbacks in a downtrend. If the
  range is tight and volume is dead → HOLD.

## `close` — into the bell (last ~30–60 min)
- **Goal:** trade the closing drive / positioning, or flatten.
- **Candles:** late-day momentum, whether the day is being bought or sold into
  the close, position vs the day's range.
- **Decide:** short-horizon momentum trades only, tight stops. Prefer HOLD if the
  move is already extended.
